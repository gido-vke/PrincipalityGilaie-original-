On this page you will find current developments within the Gilaïan government and of national importance. We strive to keep the site as complete, correct, current, and user-friendly as possible. Defense Minister uses too many intoxicants on foreign mission and attempts to raid French Military Base
Argentat (France), AUGUST 24 2021   

The Minister of Defense, Lord Paardekooper, reportedly overdosed on alcoholic substances whilst on a diplomatic mission abroad. Whilst under the influence of these yet unnamed sedatives, the defense official single-handedly made his way on protected French military grounds. Sources state that Paardekooper was unable to cross a fence that kept him from attacking the base. It it believed that his intoxication by mainly alcohol was the cause of this unusual impairment. HM the Potentate commented on the matter in a statement:

"What Lord Paardekooper was doing was incredibly brave. I applaud his dedication to the nation. I see nothing but a man that is ready to fight for Gilaïe, even when under the influence."
- His Majesty the Potentate of Gilaïe
Gendarmerie Nationale, the French authority whose base was invaded refuses to comment on the matter. It is believed that internally this breach of security is treated with diligence and caution. The Potentate has already outruled sanctions for Paardekooper. His usage of narcotics and other sedating substances will be investigated by the Ministry of Defense.
The current summer recess of the council lasts another two weeks; the Minister of Defense will continue his diplomatic mission for another week before he returns to enjoy one more week of vacation.

Passport applications opened together with new integration exam
Goes, AUGUST 20 2021    |     Lady E.C. Wagenaar

As of today, all applying for a Gilaïan citizenship must follow the guidelines described by the integration law. In order to be able to apply for citizenship, one must first complete the CIEG exam (the Civic Integration Exam for the Gilaïan citizenship). This exam consist of 25 questions regarding the suitability of one’s citizenship. After a successful completion of the exam one can be able to function in modern Gilaïe, as well as apply for the passport. As soon as the passport has been legally registered, one can officially say that they posses the Gilaïan nationality. Read more
