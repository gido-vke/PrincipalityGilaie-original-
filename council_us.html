THE GOVERNMENT



The Council of Ministers

The Council of Ministers conisists of the 10 ministers who govern our country. The minister of General and Interior Affairs is His Majesty the Potentate. He is able to combine His role as Supreme Ruler and member of the Council incredibly well. This structure allows for a much more capable controlling function as well as staying on top of things.

The Council of Ministers is the main legislative organ of the Principality of Gilaïe. Next to that, each minister also leads their own respectable ministry (executive branch). Some might call this a bad application of the Trias Politica; Our Potentate calls it a much more effective way of executing totalitarian policy. Each member of the Council is highly skilled and deemed the perfect candidate for the function. Specialisation allows for a more productive and fruitful goverment.

The Government communicates almost always through instant messenger services. Private conversations, group discussions and even voting on law-proposals. The closeness of the group nicely balances with the polical differences that inevitably occur. Next to online communication, good old-fashioned letters are still used, albeit rarely. The coronavirus pandemic and general geographical differences make it difficult for the Council to meet in person often, but when it does happen it is a great experience for every elected member.

The Secretary of State (or: State Secretary of General and Interior Affairs) is the left hand of our Potentate. She is not part of the Council and is not allowed to vote over new laws. However, she does work on important nation-wide issues and tries to maintain a subjective view that will always benefit our leader.
