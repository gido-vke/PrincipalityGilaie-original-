Gilaïe is a micronation that was established in June 2021, with the goal to provide our citizens with a government that actually cares about them. With a government consisting of three times as many people who actually live on Fugloy- we are proud to say that each citizen gets the attention, care, and safety that they deserve. Our government pledges allegiance to His Majesty the Supreme Ruler of Gilaïe. In the island’s native tongue, he is a ‘Vorst,’ to which ‘Potentate’ is a faithful translation. His Majesty also bears the title Lord of the Hougun Manor Estate. He acquired this in early 2021.


The Principality of Gilaïe believes that it complies with all the rights and obligations of a State, as laid out in the Montevideo Convention of 1933:
(a) a permanent population;
(b) a demarcated area;
(c) public authorities; and
(d) the ability to establish relations with the other States. On the basis of these basic principles, we ask every other state to grant recognition to the Principality of Gilaïe
