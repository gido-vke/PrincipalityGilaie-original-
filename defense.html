DEFENSE



The Ministry of Defense plays a huge role in protecting our nation and provides the military forces needed to deter war and ensure our nation's security. We assist the Potentate in exercising his powers and everything inherent to national defense and relations with the Armed Forces. We fight for a world where people can live in freedom and security.

Join the Military!

At the age of 17 Gilaïan inhabitants receive a letter that they are obligated to serve in the military for at least one year. This can be in either the army or navy (For joining the Air force more training is required). In times of need, people from 17 to 21 can be drafted into the military, see "Wet voor Invoering Dienstplicht onder Jongeren" on the subpage 'laws.' Of course, the military forces do not just exist out of people between the range of 17 and 21 years old. Professional soldiers work full time keeping our country safe!

You can always join the military forces, even of you are not between the ages of 17 and 21 years old. This can simply be done by sending an email or via the button below. When you enlist, you will follow the same course as all drafted civilians. In this way you will be led and supported to become perfectly trained for the armed forces.

Protection of His Majesty the Potentate of Gilaïe

Our task as defence is to protect the nation, the people and especially the Potentate. When the Potentate is out in public during special occasions, for example, the national days like Proclamation Day and Day of the Ruler, he will be protected by a small group of highly trained armed forces.

Special occasions

On the Day of the Ruler, we start with an open ceremony in the church of Kirkja. After that, we will march over the island behind the carriage with our leader in it. This march towards Hattarvík is accompanied by armed forces staying in close proximity to the carriage to prevent any worst-case scenario. When arrived at the main hall located in Hattarvík, the Potentate is guided into the hall for the closed meeting.

On the 10th of June, on Proclamation Day, the Potentate appears on the balcony of the city Hall in Hattarvík to celebrate the independence of the principality of Gilaïe. He will speak the yearly speech in which he speaks to the big crowd in front of him about the love and the unity of the nation. This event is closed on all sides by security and fences to protect the people and the Potentate at all costs. Each visitor is checked in advance in to make sure this important day is not interrupted in any way. It is not necessary to protect our supreme leader in large amounts since he is the most loved amongst the people from Gilaïe.

Everyday operations

When our ruler is not out in public, the Potentate is free to move over the island. There is no need to protect him in his spare time and he can move freely over the island. The Potentate always can ask for private guards, which will protect him undercover when he feels the need for it. This protection can come from near or watchmen from further away. This might cause him to feel freer in his spare time. When the situation escalates and the danger is rising, the same armed group as on national holidays will protect him. During international missions or occasional stays abroad, the Potentate receives the exact treatment.



We as the Gilaïan Defense Force ascertain the safety of our leader under any circumstance!
